How is Bean Dreams different from other coffee subscriptions?

Bean Dreams sends you 4oz. samples of coffee beans so you can taste before you buy.

Bean Dreams partners with coffee startups from across the country so you can find your new favorite roastery.

Bean Dreams partners with roasteries that participate in fair-trade.

How it works

First, complete a short flavor profile. We'll get a sense for what you like based on what you've bought before.

Second, we send you 4oz. sample bags of coffee beans, based on your flavor profile.

Then, opt in or out to order the beans we sent you. If you're not ready to buy, we'll send you more samples.
